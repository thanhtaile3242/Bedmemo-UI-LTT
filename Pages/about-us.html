About Bedmemo

Based on the scientific mnemonic method

Bedmemo is an app that uses the spaced repetition method to help users learn and retain information. The app allows users to create their own folders of multiple-choice questions, and then Bedmemo will remind them to review the questions at a fixed interval time.

This helps users to gradually learn the material over time, and to avoid forgetting it.
